How It Works

Professional workflow blueprint

A generic model for consulting, inspection, assessment, and reporting teams.

1. Capture Professional Input

Capture information directly from real work moments so nothing gets lost between site activity and reporting.

  • Consultations and client interviews
  • Site inspections and walkthrough notes
  • Voice notes, typed notes, photos, videos, and document uploads
  • Checklists, observations, findings, and risk annotations

2. Train

Configure the assistant using reusable skills so outputs follow your professional standards and domain language.

  • Add organization-specific skills and references
  • Upload templates, procedures, and quality rules
  • Tune output style by audience and report type

3. Trust

Review and verify every draft with traceability before final release.

  • Check extracted facts and workflow logic
  • Apply professional review and sign-off
  • Maintain accountability across team outputs
Results

4. Reporting

Generate polished report outputs, then refine with detailed editing and formatting controls.

  • Section-by-section editing and rewriting
  • Tone and audience adjustments (technical, client, executive)
  • Template-driven formatting and Word-based final polishing
